Google URL Shortener access

With Vrome, You could use <C-y> to copy shortened url of current page to your clipboard.
After grant Vrome access to your Google URL Shortener, Shortened url could be show on your dashboard at http://goo.gl
But if you haven't grant the permission, You still could shorten url. The only different is it won't be shown on your goo.gl dashboard.
